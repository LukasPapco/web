How to enjoy writing your thesis

When I was thinking about my diploma thesis topic, I wanted it to be original and recent. A lot of people take their diploma thesis as some formal necessity — something that you need to do to receive the final certificate, but I feel that this is not a good approach. I picked a topic about short term rental, because I was working in this industry and I had an interest to look at it in a bit “scientific way”. However, to choose a right topic does not necessary mean that you will enjoy writing about it.

Creating a research paper can frighten a lot of students. We are scared that we will use wrong method for hypothesis testing or at worst we will choose wrong citation norm. To overcome this fear, we need to compensate it with some positive motivation. For instance, I am using web scraping for data collecting and programming for data analyzing. It is something that I am interested in and want to improve..

So, while choosing a topic, you should also think about some complementary tools or methods which will give you a small dopamine reward and make writing thesis actually enjoyable. It can be surveys design, deep interviews, focus groups, omnibus survey or you can use some visualizing software to make your tables and graphs look good, like Tableau or Power Bi, you can train your time management skills or planning and at last but not least you can focus on your writing skills. Small tasks and accomplishment during writing your thesis are very important to create a sustainable environment. Professional use of methods and tools will also make your thesis much more valuable and it is much easier to talk and present your work.
